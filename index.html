How XBCA Works

At our core, we’re a student-powered engine. We recruit aspiring designers, developers, and strategists from campus. They join live projects, collaborate across disciplines, and deliver agency-grade outcomes — all while learning in public.

This model creates a feedback loop where clients get fresh, innovative work — and students get tangible experience, mentorship, and compensation. Everyone wins.

Why XBCA?

Our inexperience is our edge — we design with fresh perspective and no ego. We're not tied to tradition. We’re agile, curious, and unafraid to try something different. Clients come to us for boldness, not bureaucracy.

The XBCA Vision

  • Phase 1: Assemble core team, pilot early-stage projects, refine delivery workflow.
  • Phase 2: Launch student recruitment drive, onboard real clients, create in-house design systems.
  • Phase 3: Build out a platform for self-serve onboarding (both clients and talent), scale operations.
